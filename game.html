The Development History of Domestic Anime
Martial arts culture
The story of Jianwang 3 draws on historical facts from the Tang Dynasty in China, with An Lushan's attempt to conquer the Central Plains during the reign of Emperor Xuanzong as the backdrop, presenting players with a vivid and grand traditional martial arts world. The game constructs a complex martial arts landscape.
Since its launch in 2009, the number of players in Sword Online 3 has continued to grow and its popularity remains high, Around 2010, during the rising period of game development, the number of players increased significantly, and a large number of players flooded into the game, jointly building a lively and extraordinary gaming world. Developed players' deep immersion and loyalty to the game world. Its social system allows players to establish close connections in the game, forming a unique gaming social culture. Many players meet like-minded friends, gather offline, and organize guild activities through the game, extending gaming socialization from online to offline.
The rise of web games
Referring to the popular anime page games at that time, such as "Tantantang", it explains the role of the convenience of page games in the popularization of anime games, as well as its playing characteristics and market response. As a casual competitive web game, "Dan Dan Tang" quickly became popular around 2010. Players can participate in battles through simple operations, and its cute character images and rich prop system are deeply loved by players, becoming a dark horse in the web game market
Foreign anime Games
Dragon Valley
Dragon Valley brings Chinese players an unprecedented gaming experience with its 3D unlocked action combat mode. Players can freely control the movement and perspective of their characters, and flexibly dodge and attack during battles, with a tight and exciting combat rhythm. The exquisite Korean style of painting, with cute characters and fantastic scenes, is loved by anime players.
DNF
DNF has become popular in China with its side scrolling combat gameplay. Its unique combo system allows players to perform magnificent combos through operational skills, satisfying their pursuit of combat satisfaction. Rich and diverse career settings, such as Ghost Swordsman, sharpshooter, etc., each profession has exclusive skills and growth paths, providing players with diverse gaming experiences.
League of Legends
League of Legends, which entered the Chinese market around 2010, revolutionized Chinese esports and gaming culture with its fair play and rich hero settings. Its 5V5 battle mode tests teamwork and strategic planning, stimulates players' enthusiasm for competition, and promotes an unprecedented surge in the domestic esports atmosphere. The establishment of a professional league system has cultivated a large number of professional players and event commentators, promoting the standardization and professionalization of China's esports industry.
